# SudoSodoku support

SudoSodoku 2.0 is available free on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/sudosodoku/id6779813086) for iPhone running iOS 17 or later.

## Quick help

### My puzzle or timer disappeared

An active puzzle is saved locally as you play. Relaunch the app and open `archives` to resume it. The play clock counts active time only and pauses while the app is in the background.

### Game Center is not showing my profile

SudoSodoku works without Game Center. For leaderboards and achievements:

1. Open **Settings → Game Center** on your iPhone.
2. Confirm that you are signed in.
3. Return to SudoSodoku and open `whoami`.

Score and achievement delivery may wait until the device is online. If Apple’s Game Center service is unavailable, your local game and rating still work.

### My leaderboard time did not improve

Game Center retains the best eligible time for each difficulty. Only completed, independently played puzzles submit a time. Viewing a saved solution does not submit a score.

### I found a conflicting number

A conflict means the same digit appears twice in a row, column, or 3×3 box. SudoSodoku has no lives or fail state: clear the value or use undo and continue.

### I need less motion

Enable **Settings → Accessibility → Motion → Reduce Motion**. SudoSodoku provides an immediate or simplified path for its animations. The puzzle remains fully playable without motion or haptics.

## Data and privacy

Gameplay data stays on your device. There is no SudoSodoku account, analytics, advertising, tracking, or developer-operated cloud service. Game Center is optional and operated by Apple.

- Read the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).
- To remove local gameplay data, delete the app. This cannot be undone.
- Deleting the app does not delete Game Center leaderboard scores or achievements; Apple manages that data through Game Center.

Never post personal information, Apple Account details, or private Game Center information in a public issue or discussion.

## Ask a question

Search or open a thread in [GitHub Discussions Q&A](https://github.com/SudoSodokuApp/SudoSodoku/discussions/categories/q-a). Include your SudoSodoku version, iOS version, and iPhone model when they are relevant.

## Report a bug

Use the [bug report form](https://github.com/SudoSodokuApp/SudoSodoku/issues/new/choose) for a reproducible problem. Please include:

- what you expected and what happened;
- clear reproduction steps;
- app version, iOS version, and iPhone model;
- a screenshot or screen recording when useful, with personal information removed.

Security vulnerabilities must not be posted publicly. Follow the private reporting process in [.github/SECURITY.md](.github/SECURITY.md).
